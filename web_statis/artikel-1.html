The Legends Vision Canada: A triumph of legacy and inspiration




From July 6th to July 9th, The Legends' Vision World Tour swept through three major Canadian cities, leaving an indelible mark on the country's badminton landscape. Toronto, Vancouver, and Calgary played host to these impactful events, bringing together badminton legends from around the world for a mesmerizing display of skill and junior growth.


The legends journey kicked off with clinics in the coastal cities of Toronto and Vancouver. As the legendary players stepped on the courts to showcase their skills, they took the time to impart their wisdom to aspiring badminton enthusiasts. In each city, interactive coaching clinics were organized, allowing junior players to receive personalized guidance from some of the sport's greatest icons. From mastering technique to understanding strategic gameplay, the young talents were exposed to a wealth of knowledge that would undoubtedly shape their future in badminton.


The Legends' Vision events were not solely about improving technical skills; they were also focused on instilling the values of sportsmanship, determination, and perseverance. Peter Gade (DEN), Lee Chong Wei (MAS), and Lee Yong Dae (KOR), shared their personal journeys, underscoring the importance of resilience and hard work in achieving success on and off the court. These life lessons left a profound impact on the young minds, encouraging them to dream big and pursue their passion for badminton with unwavering dedication.


The final leg of the Legends' Vision took place in Calgary, where the legends gathered together for one last clinic. The World Tours final event unfolded during the YONEX Canada Open finals, where the legends played amongst other current top athletes in a fun and enthusiastic exhibition style event. The city's vibrant atmosphere resonated with the cheers of enthusiastic spectators, as they witnessed timeless entertainment brought to them by the sports greatest names.


the Yonex Legends' Vision events were not only a celebration of the sport's legendary icons but also a platform to cultivate the next generation of badminton stars. Through interactive clinics and community-focused programs, the tournament left an indelible mark on young players, inspiring them to pursue their dreams and embrace the values of sportsmanship, determination, and camaraderie. The legacy of the Legends' Vision will undoubtedly thrive as these aspiring players carry the torch of badminton into the future.
